Meet: Sapna Yadav

I offer a safe environment for children, to be themselves, to explore, to get positive strokes.

0 to 5 yrs is an important foundation age for children. They need to feel good about themselves and the people they are surrounded with. I lead them gently so they pick up a routine and learn to be helpful and kind.

I also spot their strengths and encourage them in that direction. Children feel supported, and then they get ready for school life.

About our activities

At our family daycare, our kids engage in a various range of activities:

  • Painting
  • Yoga
  • Dancing
  • Weekly excursions (to a nearby park)
  • Games

Art and games encourage youngsters to examine creativity more deeply, think about it, and discuss it. They provide a framework for faculties to build on children's observations, understandings, and discussions about creativity.
